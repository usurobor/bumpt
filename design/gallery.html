The look is one idea: cohering. The logo is a single ring — whole at rest (the room before a bump), drawing itself into one when something coheres. Bone-on-black with hairlines is the resting quiet; the one accent, coherence violet, appears only at the moment of a bump — the mark, the action, the count — and nowhere else.
